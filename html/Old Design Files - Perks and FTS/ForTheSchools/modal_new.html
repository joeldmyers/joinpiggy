What’s should I do next?

We always recommend Sharing or Shopping, whatever you’re in the mood for! Every time you shop with one of our partners, your school earns money. No catches, no cost, no gimmicks. You shop, we pay your school. Just tell us which school should benefit from your shopping. You do that by clicking the connect button below or by installing the shopping button above.

Do I need to create an account?

Not if you Never, Ever delete your cookies or switch computers. As long as you tell us which school you’d like to support before you shop or install the shopping button, you do not need an account. Creating an account is a great way to track your shopping and donations made to your school AND get alerts and updates on promotions, sales and store coupons.

What does it cost?

It’s Totally Free! Nothing for you, the school, the PTA, or any groups. No set up charges, no membership dues... it’s completely free. We are paid by our advertisers, and we share that with your school or group. We are paid in cash, and we pay your group in cash, not points.

How much does my School/PTA get paid?

That varies by merchant. It’s almost always a percentage of your purchase, sometimes it’s a fixed amount... it just depends on the merchant. The percentage donated to your school is listed with every merchant.
