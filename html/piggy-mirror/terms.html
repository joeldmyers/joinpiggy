Earning Cash Back

Earning a portion of your purchases as cash back (“Cash Back”) is simple. Begin your shopping at the Website or with one of our Applications and select the store, product, coupon or other offer which interests you. Click on the link provided, and you’ll be directed to an affiliated store’s website (“Affiliate Store”). Complete a purchase and you’ll earn Cash Back on your net purchase amount. You may also earn Cash Back on purchases made ‘in-store’ at a limited number of stores by presenting a JoinPiggy.com coupon at time of check out. Such stores and coupons are clearly identified on the Website. Cash Back amounts differ by store and are provided in detail at the Website, and may be changed at any time, with or without notice. Please note that the net purchase amount for determining Cash Back excludes taxes, fees, shipping, gift-wrapping, discounts or credits, returns or cancellations, and extended warranties. In addition, certain Affiliate Stores may exclude certain products and purchases from the Program; our goal is to limit the number of such exclusions and restrictions and to maintain an up-to-date list of such exclusions and conditions on the Website.

To earn Cash Back, you must complete your purchase during the same shopping session you start after leaving the Website and visiting the Affiliate Store. If you visit other sites before completing your purchase or use coupons by another site or service, your purchase may be linked with a service other than JoinPiggy.com and you may not earn cash back on your purchase from JoinPiggy.com. Please be sure to enable cookies on your computer. If you disable ‘cookies’ on your computer, you will not earn Cash Back as cookies are used to authenticate the user and be certain Cash Back is assigned to the Member account.

Upon registration of your user name with JoinPiggy.com, an account will be set up in your name for all Cash Back you earn. The appropriate Cash Back rebate will be posted to your account as “Reported” within thirty (30) days of the date on which an Affiliate Store reports your transaction to JoinPiggy.com. For a period of between ninety-one (91) and one hundred twenty-one (121) days thereafter, your account will refer to the Cash Back rebate as “Pending.” This time period is in accordance with the rights of the Affiliate Store to reverse a transaction for return purposes. Once the Affiliate Store authorizes payment to JoinPiggy.com of the fees payable regarding a qualifying purchase made by you, JoinPiggy.com will credit your account with the applicable Cash Back. Your Cash Back earned will thereafter be identified as “Available,” and eligible to be redeemed, subject to the terms of this Agreement. You can view your account status and recent earning history at any time via the My Perks tab on the Website.
