How soon do Purchases Show My Cash Back Earned?

Usually by the next day. Some stores are instantaneous, and when they share the information about your purcahse our servers will automatically apply that to your account. Some stores will report the next business day after your order has been fulfilled (i.e. shipped). Some Travel & Special Order sites for Tickets will report cash back after you have completed your stay, etc.

Is something not showing up after the above times? Please let us know!

How Do I get My Bonus or Referral Rewards?

Once the eligible criteria is met for the bonus or reward, we will total that Earned amount and you will see a "Bonuses" tab appear above next to the Earnings & Payments. An example is referral bonuses are paid when the person you refer earns the cash back & it's a locked status. If they earn $25; you will see a $5 bonus! As they earn more, your referral rewards & bonuses will grow!

When are payments sent?

Payments will be sent once youe cash back balances have transistioned to the "locked" phase. This is usually once past the stores return policy. Any returns made will negate or lower the cash back earned.

Once your balance of Locked & Earned cash back reaches $50, we will send you a check to the address listed on your account! Checks are sent Quarterly, in March, June, September, and December for the current avaiable earnings at the time of processing payments.

Wondering what "Locked" means? Basically once the merchant is sure your order is complete & youre not returning items, it will shift into "locked." As you can imagine, with the Thousands of Stores we have, it's hard to put a general timeline on that. It's usually reflected by the Store's return policy + 15-30 days to process them!
