PTA & PTO's

More than Fundraising

Headlines like those on the right are becoming all too common. They're one of the reasons we created ForTheSchools.com, to ensure that our kids have the same opportunities for a quality education that we did. But ForTheSchools isn't just about fundraising for schools, it's also giving you the tools to help engage parents and increase their involvement and volunteerism.

Parental Involvement

We live in a busy world and communication is becomming increasingly difficult. FoTheSchools provides groups with one central location to communicate, promote, and foster parental involvement… and it's FREE.

ForTheSchools.com gives your group it's own webpage designed to promote upcoming events, volunteer opportunities, build relationships with your parents, and help you monitor your fundraising goals. Even if your group already has a website, we can still help you with our free apps and soon to be launched site widget. Contact us for more information!

Group Connector

As your groups administrator, we enable you to create and manage multiple groups for your school. We recognize that no two schools are exactly alike and you'll find that with the flexibility built into our systems, you'll easily be able to manage or assign manangement for as many groups as you have, from the football boosters to the band parents, all of your groups can be connected and you will decide how. And like everything else with ForTheSchools, it's FREE.
