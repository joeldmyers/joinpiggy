PTA & PTO's

Automatic Fundraising

One of the primary purposes of any PTO/PTA is to fundraise. Your parents and firends already shop online. ForTheSchools helps your group fund raise each time they do... Automatically!

Fundraising is very competitive, not to mention time consuming. It seems like every time the phone rings or we walk into a grocery store, someone is soliciting for a donation. After a while, we become immune to their pleas. ForTheSchools.com is different. We provide you with the tools to create a new revenue stream. Each time one of your parents or friends makes a purchase online with one of our merchants, we pay your group. You don't have to do anything.
