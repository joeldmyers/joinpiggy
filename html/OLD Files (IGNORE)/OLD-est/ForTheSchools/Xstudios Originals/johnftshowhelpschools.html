How We Help Schools

A New Way

The pool for charitable giving is not a bottomless pit. It never has been. It seems though that now more than ever many great causes are feeling their budgets tighten and are forced to make cuts we and they wish they didn't need to.

One day we had an idea. What if we could find money outside the pool of dollars that are allocated to charity? What if we could find a way for schools and children to benefit from activities that their parents already do?

It's Automatic

Americans will spend over $250,000,000,000 shopping online this year. The average parent of school age children spends over $800 per year online. Our customers average about 8% cash back on their purchases.

That means if your school has 400 parents, you can generate around $25,000 of additional funding for your school and/or support organizations without selling a single roll of gift wrap or soliciting a donation. It just happens. Automatically!

And There's More...

It gets better. From now until September 30th, we are giving schools the opportunity to win up to $25,000. One school, the one receiving the most votes, will win $5,000... guaranteed. The five schools with the next highest vote total will each win $1,000... guaranteed.

At the end of the contest, we'll give one voter a chance to win $25,000 for their child's college education. The school they voted for will win another $25,000. At ForTheSchools, we really mean it when we say that everybody wins!
